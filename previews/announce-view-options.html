Announce view — design options

Four possible layouts for the Announce composer, all on the frosted translucent surface used by the Weather & Welcome views. Toggle Light/Dark to check both themes. Mock-up only — pick a direction (or mix & match pieces) and I'll build it.

Option A · Refined single column

Closest to today, cleaned up: frosted surface, each section in its own sub-panel, an explicit Everyone chip, Rooms/Devices grouped with online dots, preset tiles, and a send button that summarises the target. Custom text hidden behind a “Custom…” toggle.

Option C · Preset-forward (big tiles)

The predefined messages are the hero — large tiles you tap to arm. A slim target bar and the mode + send sit underneath. Fast for the everyday “tap the message, hit send” flow.

Option D · Compact “sentence” form — adaptive Recent

The tight, form-like composer (Say [message] to [targets], [mode]), with Recent that repositions itself: a side rail when the card is wide enough, otherwise a collapsed section below the composer. Both states are shown here (it's a real container query — resize your browser and the wide one flips to the narrow layout).
