Navbar — popup / overflow trigger styles

The chevron on the right is the popup (or auto-overflow) trigger. Below are three ways it can look next to the regular Home / list / remote buttons. Same icons & theme as your screenshot.

A · Make it match the others recommended

No box, no border — a transparent button with the accent icon colour, sized like the other buttons. The chevron blends in as just another icon.

B · Keep the box, fix the colour

Keeps the surface box & border (so it still reads as a "menu"), but switches the chevron from muted grey to the accent colour.

C · Leave it as-is current

Today's look: a filled grey surface box with a border and a muted-grey chevron — the deliberate "menu affordance" that stands out from the bare icon buttons.
